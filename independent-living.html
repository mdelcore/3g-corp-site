Independent Living

Benefits

  • Housing options for senior citizens who desire to live on their own, with a maintenance free lifestyle, while allowing them to enjoy all the amenities we have to offer
  • We strive to provide all our residents top notch service
  • Live surrounded by people who share the same interests and activities
  • Provides assistance to residence in transition to another level of care

Amenities

  • Health and wellness programs
  • Unique healthy and flavorful meal options
  • Monthly events to keep one active enjoying the things they like to do

THE GOAL OF INDEPENDENT LIVING AT A 3G HEALTHCARE COMMUNITY IS ALLOWING OUR RESIDENTS TO BE WHO THEY ARE DOING THE THINGS THEY LOVE!
